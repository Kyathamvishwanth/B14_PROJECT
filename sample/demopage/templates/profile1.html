How It Works

We're here to help you. Follow these steps to upload your image securely:

  1. Ensure your image clearly shows the skin area in question.
  2. Click the "Choose File" button below to select your image.
  3. Once you've selected an image, click "Submit."
  4. Our system will analyze your image and provide feedback.

Rest assured, your privacy is important to us, and all images are handled securely.
